5 a.m. _ Demonstrators, many walking hand-in-hand, begin leaving the square, ending their three-week occupation.
Students are told by loudspeaker that troops will move in and clear the area.
4:30 a.m. _ Armored personnel carriers enter the square.
3:30 a.m. _ Military units surround the square, trapping several thousand students inside.
2 a.m. _ Thousands of troops arrive at the west side of the square and some of the riflemen open fire.
A smaller group of about 1,000 military personnel enters the square from the east and shooting erupts.
More people shot as troops fire at crowds.
1 a.m. Sunday.
12 midnight _ Armored vehicles break through barricades and about 40 trucks carrying military personnel slowly advance toward the square from the west.
