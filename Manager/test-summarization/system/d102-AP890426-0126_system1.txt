``I remember she once gave me an Indian tepee, then my own dressing room,'' he said.
He said Miss Ball often gave him gifts and that he remembers spending at least one Christmas at her home.
Actually, she was just the opposite and was very serious.
She had her ways but despite all, she was a great person, very, very talented.
``And I spent more time with her off the set at her home than at the studio,'' he said.
``I signed a seven-year contract with the studio in 1956 and the first episode I was in was aired that December with Bob Hope _ I just turned 6.''
