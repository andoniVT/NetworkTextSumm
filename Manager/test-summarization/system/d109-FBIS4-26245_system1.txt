In the drought-stricken areas, 12 million people and 12 million head of livestock are short of drinking water, according to the State Flood Control and Drought Relief Headquarters.
The State Flood Control and Drought Relief Headquarters has sent special teams of people to help fight floods in Hunan and Jiangxi.
The Jiangxi provincial party committee and people's government are mobilizing local people and the troops stationed there to combat the floods.
More than half a million Armymen and local residents, under the leadership of the Hunan provincial party provincial committee and provincial people's government, are going all out to fight the flood.
