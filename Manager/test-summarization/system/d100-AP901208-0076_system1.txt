Peebles said late in 1980 that Lennon had just recovered from a period when he had ``gone off the rails'' and his relationship with Ms. Ono had suffered.
Journalist Andy Peebles, the last person to interview Lennon before he was shot, said in an interview with Sky satellitetelevision news: ``Ten years on, it's a ghastly feeling to think that you had spent ... seven hours ... with somebody you have been a fan of for years, and I think I left ... thinking `This is great, he's in really good shape.'''
