Temperatures around the nation at 3 p.m. EST ranged from 7 degrees at Fergus Falls, Minn., to 88 degrees at Miami, Fla.

Cold weather was expected to invade the Southeast, with warnings of freezing overnight temperatures posted for Friday morning in Georgia, Alabama, northwest Florida, most of Mississippi and all but extreme southeast Louisiana.
